Cinnamon Rolls

Heres a simple step-by-step guide to making cinnamon rolls using an air fryer, perfect for a busy college student living in a dorm!:


Ingredients:

  • 1 can of refrigerated cinnamon rolls((like Pillsbury)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter( (melted)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of cinnamon (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sugar:(optional, for extra sweetness)
  • Frosting:(usually included with the cinnamon roll package)

Materials:

  • Air fryer
  • Spoon and plate
  • Microwave-safe bowl or container (to heat up the steak if necessary)

Steps:

  1. Gather Your Ingredients:
      -Get everything ready: Open the can of refrigerated cinnamon rolls and have your butter, cinnamon, and sugar at hand.

  2. Prepare the Cinnamon Rolls:
      -Separate: Take the cinnamon rolls out of the can and separate them.
      -Mix cinnamon and sugar (optional): In a small bowl, combine the cinnamon and sugar if you want a sweeter, spiced flavor.

  3. Preheat the Air Fryer:
      -Preheat: If your air fryer requires preheating, set it to 350F (175C) for about 3-5 minutes.

  4. Prepare the Rolls:
      -Melt the butter: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in the microwave (about 15-30 seconds).
      -Brush the butter: Use a spoon or brush to spread melted butter on the tops of the cinnamon rolls.
      Add cinnamon sugar (optional): Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture over the buttered tops for extra flavor.

  5. Air Fry the Cinnamon Rolls:
      6. -Arrange: Place the cinnamon rolls in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Make sure they have a little space between them to allow for rising.
      Cook: Set the air fryer to cook at 350F (175C) for about 8-10 minutes. Check on them halfway through to ensure theyre cooking evenly.

  6. Frost and Serve:
      7. - Check for doneness: Once they are golden brown and cooked through, carefully remove them from the air fryer (theyll be hot!).
      Frost: Drizzle the included frosting over the warm cinnamon rolls. If the frosting is too thick, you can microwave it for a few seconds to make it easier to spread.
      Enjoy: Serve your delicious cinnamon rolls on a plate and enjoy!

  7. Tips:
      8. -Keep an eye on the cinnamon rolls while they cook, as air fryers can vary in temperature and cooking times.
      -OStore any leftovers in an airtight container for a quick snack later!

Enjoy your easy and tasty cinnamon rolls!
