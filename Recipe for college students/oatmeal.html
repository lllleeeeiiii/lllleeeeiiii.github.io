Oatmeal

Here’s a step-by-step guide to making microwave oatmeal in your dorm:


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats (rolled or quick oats)
  • 1 cup milk (or water for a lighter option)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Fresh fruit (like berries or a sliced banana)
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds (optional)

Materials:

  • Microwave (to cook the oatmeal)
  • Microwave-safe bowl (for mixing and cooking the oats)
  • Spoon
  • Knife (cutting fruit if needed)
  • Measuring cups

Steps:

  1. Prepare the oats:
      - Place 1/2 cup oats in a microwave-safe bowl. Make sure the bowl is large enough to prevent overflow while cooking.

  2. Add liquid:
      -Pour 1 cup of milk (or water) over the oats. Stir to combine.

  3. Microwave:
      -Microwave the mixture on high for 2-3 minutes. Keep an eye on it to make sure it doesn’t spill over.
      -If using rolled oats, you might need to stir halfway through and cook a little longer.

  4. Stir and check consistency:
      -Once done, stir the oatmeal. If it’s too thick, add a splash of milk or water to reach your desired consistency.

  5. Add toppings:
      6. -Drizzle 1 tablespoon of honey for sweetness.
      -Add your choice of fresh fruit like berries or sliced banana.
      -Sprinkle 1 teaspoon chia seeds for extra fiber and nutrients.

  6. Enjoy:
      7. -Stir everything together and enjoy your healthy, dorm-friendly breakfast! Feel free to customize with your favorite toppings like nuts, cinnamon, or nut butter.
