Ezo Kitchen + Sushi Bar


Welcome to Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar, a culinary retreat nestled in the heart of Long Island’s suburbs. Founded by Michael, a chef inspired by his extensive travels throughout Asia, Ezo blends authentic Asian cuisine with a contemporary twist, right in your neighborhood. Michael, though not of Asian descent, brings a respectful and collaborative approach to his craft. He has worked closely with chefs from Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand to create a menu that reflects genuine flavors and cultural traditions, ensuring each dish is as authentic as it is delicious. Our focus is on sushi, thoughtfully crafted to satisfy both enthusiasts and newcomers, with flavors and textures that are designed to excite your palate. In addition to sushi, we offer a curated selection of Vietnamese and Thai dishes, offering a unique journey through the diverse flavors of Asia. From a cozy lunch spot to a refined dinner destination, Ezo is here to be a local favorite, welcoming suburban families and food enthusiasts alike. We’ve crafted a sleek, inviting space that blends sophistication with a bit of flair, mirroring Michael’s commitment to timeless design and cultural respect. At Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar, we’re more than just a restaurant; we’re a bridge to the flavors of Asia, served with genuine passion and a touch of local charm. Join us for a dining experience that celebrates tradition, respect, and unforgettable taste.
