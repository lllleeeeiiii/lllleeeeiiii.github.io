Salmon Over Roce

Here's a super easy and quick recipe for making salmon over rice perfect for a college student on the go!


Ingredients:

  • 1 salmon filet(pre-cooked, canned, or microwavable for convenience)
  • 1 cup of instant rice or pre-cooked rice(microwavable rice packets work too)
  • 1/2 cup grapes (red or green),(optional)
  • Soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, or lemon juice((for flavor)
  • Olive oil or butter(optional, for flavor)
  • Optional toppingssteamed veggies, avocado, or sesame seeds

Materials:

  • 1 microwave-safe bowl or rice cooker (for cooking rice)
  • 1 microwave-safe plate or pan (if using stovetop)
  • 1 fork or spatula

Steps:

  1. Cook the Rice:
      -Microwave Option: If using microwavable rice, follow the instructions on the packet (usually around 1-2 minutes in the microwave).
      Instant Rice Option: If using instant rice, combine 1 cup of instant rice and 1 cup of water in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave for 1-2 minutes (or follow the directions on the package). Let it sit for 1 minute.

  2. Prepare the Salmon:
      -Microwavable Salmon: If you have a microwavable salmon fillet, place it on a microwave-safe plate, season with salt and pepper (optional), and heat it according to the package (usually 1-2 minutes).
      -Canned Salmon: Drain the can and add the salmon directly to the cooked rice.
      -Pre-cooked or Pan-Seared Salmon: If you have access to a stove, quickly heat the salmon in a pan over medium heat with a little olive oil or butter for 2-3 minutes on each side.

  3. Combine the Salmon and Rice:
      -Once the rice is cooked, place the salmon fillet (or canned salmon) on top of the rice.
      -Drizzle soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, or lemon juice over the salmon and rice for flavor.
      -Optionally, add a little olive oil or butter for extra richness.

  4. Add Toppings (optional):
      -If you have some quick veggies (like microwavable frozen veggies), you can steam them and add them on top for a balanced meal.
      -Avocado slices or a sprinkle of sesame seeds are also great add-ons for extra texture and flavor.

  5. Quick Tips:
      -Pre-cooked salmon packets: These are available at most grocery stores and make this recipe even faster.
      -Microwavable rice cups: Another huge time-saver, since they take just a minute to heat up.
      -Meal prep: Cook extra rice and store it in the fridge for quick meals later.
