Mac n Cheese

Hereâ€™s a simple step-by-step recipe for making boxed mac and cheese in a dorm room! It's quick and easy, perfect for a busy college student.


Ingredients:

  • 1 box of mac and cheese(any brand)
  • Water(for boiling)
  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine(as indicated on the box)
  • 1/4 cup milk(as indicated on the box)

Materials:

  • Microwave-safe bowl (large enough to hold pasta and water)
  • Microwave
  • Fork or spoon (for stirring)
  • Measuring cups

Steps:

  1. Pour the Pasta into the Bowl:
      -Empty the pasta from the mac and cheese box into your microwave-safe bowl.

  2. Add Water:
      -Add enough water to cover the pasta completely, with about 1 inch above the pasta level (around 2-3 cups depending on the bowl size).

  3. Microwave the Pasta:
      -Place the bowl in the microwave and cook on high for 6-8 minutes, stirring every 2-3 minutes to ensure the pasta cooks evenly. Check the pasta for doneness (it should be tender but not mushy).

  4. Drain the Pasta:
      -Once the pasta is cooked, carefully drain the excess water using a lid or a strainer (be careful, it will be hot).

  5. Add Butter:
      -While the pasta is still warm, add 2 tablespoons of butter or margarine and stir until melted and combined.

  6. Add Milk and Cheese:
      -Pour in 1/4 cup of milk and the cheese powder packet from the box. Stir everything together until the cheese sauce is creamy and smooth.

  7. Serve and Enjoy:
      -Your mac and cheese is ready! Grab a fork or spoon and enjoy it right away.


This is a super simple recipe, and you can make it in less than 15 minutes while studying! You can also add extras like cooked veggies, hot sauce, or chicken to make it more filling.
