Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt(plain or flavored)
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup fresh fruit (like berries, sliced banana, or diced apple)
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Materials:

  • Cup or bowl (for layering the parfait)
  • Spoon (for scooping and layering ingredients)
  • Measuring cups (for measuring yogurt, granola, and fruit)

Steps:

  1. Prepare your ingredients:
      -Grab a cup or bowl to layer your parfait.
      -Wash and slice your fresh fruit (if needed).

  2. First layer - Greek yogurt:
      -Spoon 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt into the bottom of your cup or bowl.

  3. Add granola:
      -Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of granola over the yogurt layer for a nice crunch.

  4. Add fresh fruit:
      - Add 1/4 cup of fresh fruit on top of the granola. You can use berries, bananas, or any fruit you have on hand.

  5. Second layer of yogurt:
      6. -Add another 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt on top of the fruit.

  6. Top with more granola and fruit:
      7. -Sprinkle the remaining 2 tablespoons of granola and the rest of your fresh fruit over the yogurt.

  7. Chia seeds (optional):
      8. -Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of chia seeds on top for extra fiber and texture.

  8. Drizzle honey:
      9. -Finish by drizzling **1 tablespoon of honey** over the parfait for sweetness.

  9. Enjoy:
      10. -Grab a spoon and enjoy your quick and healthy dorm-friendly breakfast or snack!

You can customize this parfait with different fruits, nuts, or seeds to keep it exciting!
