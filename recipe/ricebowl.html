Rice Bowl

Here’s a quick and easy rice bowl recipe you can make on the go as a busy college student, using simple ingredients and materials.


Ingredients:

  • 1 microwaveable rice packet(brown or white rice)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or tofu(pre-cooked or store-bought)
  • 1/4 cup canned corn(drained)
  • 1/4 cup canned black beans(drained and rinsed)
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon salsa or hot sauce(optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lime or lemon juice(optional, for freshness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or dressing(optional)

Materials:

  • Microwave-safe bowl
  • Spoon or fork
  • Microwave

Steps:

  1. Heat the Rice:
      -Place the microwaveable rice packet in the microwave and heat according to the package instructions (usually 1-2 minutes). Transfer the rice into a microwave-safe bowl.

  2. Add Pre-Cooked Protein:
      -If you're using pre-cooked chicken or tofu, warm it up in the microwave for 30-60 seconds. Add it on top of the rice.

  3. Add the Corn and Black Beans:
      -Drain and rinse the black beans and corn. Add about 1/4 cup of each to the rice bowl.

  4. Add Fresh Ingredients:
      -Slice up 1/4 of an avocado and place it on top of the rice. If you have any other fresh veggies (like lettuce, tomatoes, or cucumbers), feel free to toss those in too!

  5. Optional Toppings:
      -Add 1 tablespoon of salsa or hot sauce if you like a bit of spice. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top for some extra flavor. Drizzle with olive oil or your favorite dressing for added richness.

  6. Mix and Serve:
      -Gently mix everything with a spoon, ensuring all the fruits are evenly coated. Enjoy it right away, or store it in a container if you're prepping it for later.

      7. Add a pinch of salt and pepper, and mix everything together with a spoon or fork.

This rice bowl is super flexible, and you can customize it with whatever ingredients you have on hand. It’s a quick, filling, and nutritious option for a busy day!
