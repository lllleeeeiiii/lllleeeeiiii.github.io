Steak Salad

Heres a simple steak salad recipe thats perfect for a college student, using basic materials and your air fryer and microwave!


Ingredients:

  • 1 small steak (around 6 oz, like sirloin or flank steak)
  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, or your favorite salad mix)
  • 1/2 avocado,sliced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes,halved
  • 1/4 cucumber,sliced
  • 1/4 red onion,thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salad dressing of your choice(vinaigrette works well)

Materials:

  • Air fryer
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Microwave-safe bowl or container (to heat up the steak if necessary)

Steps:

  1. Season the Steak:
      -Rub your steak with a bit of olive oil (optional), salt, and pepper.

  2. Cook the Steak in the Air Fryer:
      - Preheat the air fryer to 400F (200C) for a few minutes.
      -Place the steak in the air fryer and cook for 6–10 minutes, depending on how well-done you like it. Flip halfway through.
      - Check the internal temperature with a meat thermometer if possible: Medium-rare:130–135F Medium: 135–145F Medium-well: 145–155F

  3. Rest the Steak:
      -After its done, let the steak rest on a plate for about 5 minutes. This helps the juices redistribute.

  4. Prepare the Salad:
      -In a bowl, add your mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and avocado.

  5. Slice the Steak:
      -Once rested, slice the steak thinly against the grain.

  6. Assemble the Salad:
      -Top the salad with the steak slices. Drizzle with your favorite dressing.

  7. Serve and Enjoy!:
      8. -You can microwave the steak slices for 20 30 seconds if they cool down too much while resting.

This recipe is super customizable—you can add any other veggies or even grains like quinoa or brown rice!
