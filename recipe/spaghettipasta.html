Spaghetti Pasta

Here's an easy, quick recipe for spaghetti pasta that’s perfect for a college student on the go!


Ingredients:

  • 1 serving of spaghetti(about a handful, or 2 oz)
  • Salt(optional, for the water)
  • 1 jar of your favorite pasta sauce(or microwaveable pasta sauce cup)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese(optional)
  • Olive oil or butter(optional)

Materials:

  • 1 pot for boiling pasta
  • 1 strainer (optional, or use the pot lid)
  • 1 fork or tongs
  • 1 microwave-safe bowl (if using microwave sauce)
  • Stovetop or electric burner

Steps:

  1. Boil Water:
      -Fill your pot with water (about 3/4 full).
      -Add a pinch of salt to the water if you like, to season the pasta.
      -Place the pot on the stovetop and turn the heat to high. Bring the water to a boil.
    • Cook the Spaghetti:
        -Once the water is boiling, add the spaghetti.
        -Stir the spaghetti so it doesn't stick together.
        -Let it cook for 8-10 minutes (check the package for exact cooking time). Stir occasionally to prevent it from sticking.

    • Drain the Pasta:
        -Once the pasta is cooked (taste-test for softness), carefully drain the water using a strainer or by holding the pot lid against the pasta and pouring out the water.
        If you don't have a strainer, pour slowly and carefully to avoid spills.

    • Heat the Sauce:
        -If you have pasta sauce, pour it into a microwave-safe bowl and heat it in the microwave for 30 seconds to 1 minute until warm. Stir halfway through.
        -You can also add cold sauce directly to the hot pasta if you're in a rush—it will warm up quickly.

    • Combine and Serve
        -Mix the warm sauce into your pasta.
        -Add olive oil or a small amount of butter for extra flavor, and sprinkle some Parmesan cheese on top (if desired).

    • Quick Tips:
        -Time-saving hack: Buy pre-made microwaveable pasta sauce cups to save time.
        -Meal prep idea: Cook extra pasta and store it in the fridge for future meals.
        -Flavor boost: Add garlic powder or Italian seasoning for a more flavorful dish.


Enjoy your quick and easy spaghetti! Perfect for busy days in college.
