Smoothies

Heres a quick and healthy smoothie recipe you can make in your dorm, perfect for when youre on the go!


Ingredients:

  • 1 banana (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries (like blueberries or strawberries)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (or a dairy-free alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter (or almond butter)
  • 1/2 cup milk or plant-based milk (like almond, oat, or soy milk)
  • 1 handful spinach (optional for added nutrients, but you wont taste it!)
  • Ice (optional, if you want it extra cold)

Materials:

  • Blender (to blend the smoothie)
  • Measuring cups (for measuring ingredients)
  • Measuring spoons (for measuring ingredients like peanut butter and chia seeds)
  • Knife (for slicing the banana, if needed)

Steps:

  1. Prepare your ingredients:
      -If your dorm doesnt have a fridge/freezer, you can use shelf-stable milk and single-serve packs of nut butter. Buy pre-frozen fruit or use a banana to make it creamy.

  2. Blend your smoothie:
      -Place the banana, frozen berries, Greek yogurt, peanut butter, chia seeds, spinach (if using), and milk into a blender.

  3. Blend until smooth:
      - Blend on high until smooth and creamy. If it's too thick, add a little more milk to reach your desired consistency.

  4. Add ice if needed:
      -If you like your smoothie cold and extra thick, throw in a handful of ice cubes and blend again.

  5. Pour and go:
      6. -Pour into a portable cup, and youre ready to head out the door!

You can also prep smoothie bags in your dorm by pre-measuring your ingredients (except liquid) into zip-lock bags. When you're in a rush, just dump a bag into the blender, add milk, and blend.
