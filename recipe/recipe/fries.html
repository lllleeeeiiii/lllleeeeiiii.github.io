Fries

Heres a simple step-by-step recipe for making frozen fries using an air fryer, perfect for a busy college student like you:


Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of frozen fries(your choice: regular, crinkle-cut, steak fries, etc.)
  • Optional:salt, pepper, or your favorite seasoning

Materials:

  • Air fryer
  • Bowl
  • Spatula or tongs
  • Plate or serving bowl

Steps:

  1. Preheat the Air Fryer:
      -Turn on the air fryer: Set the temperature to 400F (200C)
      -Preheat: Let it preheat for about 3-5 minutes

  2. Prepare the Fries
      -Open the bag: Take out the frozen fries from the bag.
      -Optional seasoning: If you want, you can toss the fries in a bowl with a little salt or your favorite seasoning for extra flavor.

  3. Cook the Fries:
      -Add to the bask: Place the frozen fries in the air fryer basket. Make sure not to overcrowd the basket; its best to cook them in a single layer for even crispiness.
      -Cook time: Air fry the fries for about 15-20 minutes, shaking the basket or flipping the fries halfway through to ensure they cook evenly.

  4. Check for Doneness:
      -Carefully take the fries out of the air fryer using a spatula or tongs and place them on a plate or serving bowl.

  5. Season and Serve:
      6. If desired, sprinkle a little extra salt or seasoning on top while they are still hot.

  6. Enjoy!:
      7. -Dig in: Serve with your favorite dipping sauce (like ketchup, ranch, or mayo) and enjoy your delicious, crispy fries!

Enjoy your easy, tasty fries!
