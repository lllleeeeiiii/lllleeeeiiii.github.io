HOT DOG

Hot Dog Ah, the quintessential New York hot dog! Picture this: a perfectly grilled sausage, juicy and flavorful, tucked into a soft, fresh bun. Slather on a generous squeeze of bright yellow mustard and a drizzle of rich, tangy ketchup, and you’ve got yourself a masterpiece. It’s the ultimate street food experience—quick, delicious, and oh-so-satisfying. Whether you’re grabbing one from a corner vendor or enjoying it at a Yankees game, there’s just something about that combo that screams NYC. It’s more than food; it’s a slice of life!


Ingredients

  • Hot Dogs: Look for quality beef or pork hot dogs.
  • Buns: Soft, fresh New England-style buns or classic hot dog rolls.
  • Mustard: Yellow mustard is the classic choice.
  • Ketchup: A drizzle of ketchup adds sweetness.

Instructions

  1. Grab Your Dogs: Start with quality beef or pork hot dogs.

  2. Boil or Grill: Grill them for that charred flavor or boil for 5-7 minutes.

  3. Toast the Buns: Lightly toast your New England-style buns for warmth and crunch.

  4. Dress It Up: Place the hot dog in the bun and squirt on yellow mustard.

  5. Add the Ketchup: Drizzle ketchup on top for a sweet balance.

  6. Onions & More: Add finely chopped raw onions or sautéed onion sauce.

  7. Final Touches: Consider adding relish or sauerkraut for extra flavor.

  8. Dig In: Grab a napkin and enjoy your tasty creation!
