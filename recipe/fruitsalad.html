Fruit Salad


Heres a super easy and quick fruit salad recipe thats perfect for a college student, using basic ingredients and materials!


Ingredients:

  • 1 apple,chopped
  • 1 banana,sliced
  • 1/2 cup grapes (red or green), halved
  • 1/2 cup berries(strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries)
  • 1/2 orange, peeled and segmented(optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey(optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lime or lemon juice(optional, for freshness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt(optional, for a creamy version)

Materials:

  • Knife and cutting board
  • Mixing bowl
  • Spoon
  • Small container (if prepping to-go)

Steps:

  1. Wash the Fruit:
      -Rinse all the fruits thoroughly under cold water.

  2. Chop the Fruits:
      -Peel and chop the apple into bite-sized pieces.
      - Slice the banana.
      -Cut the grapes in half.
      -If using strawberries, hull them and slice them.
      Peel and segment the orange if using it.

  3. Combine in a Bowl:
      -Place all the chopped fruits (apple, banana, grapes, berries, and orange) into a mixing bowl.

  4. Optional Sweetness:
      -Drizzle with honey if you want it a bit sweeter. Add a squeeze of lime or lemon juice to keep the fruit fresh and to add a slight tang.

  5. Optional Creamy Version:
      -Stir in a couple of tablespoons of Greek yogurt if you want a creamy fruit salad.

  6. Mix and Serve:
      -Gently mix everything with a spoon, ensuring all the fruits are evenly coated. Enjoy it right away, or store it in a container if you're prepping it for later.


This fruit salad is fresh, healthy, and perfect for a quick snack or breakfast!
