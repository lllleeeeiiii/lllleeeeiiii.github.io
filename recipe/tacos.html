Tacos


Heres an easy and quick recipe for making tacos with ground turkey, cheese, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, and taco sauce! Perfect for a busy college student!


Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • Taco seasoning packet(or salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder)
  • Sour cream(about 1/2 cup)
  • 1 tomato(diced)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • Taco sauce (to haste)
  • Olive oil or cooking spray(optional, for cooking)

Materials:

  • 1 frying pan or skillet
  • 1 spatula or wooden spoon
  • 1 microwave-safe plate (for heating taco shells if needed)
  • 1 knife (for chopping veggies)
  • 1 cutting board

Steps:

  1. Wash the Fruit:
      -Rinse all the fruits thoroughly under cold water.

  2. Cook the Ground Turkey:
      -Heat the pan: Place your frying pan on medium heat. Add a little olive oil or cooking spray if needed.
      -Cook the turkey: Add the ground turkey to the pan and break it apart using the spatula.
      -Season the turkey: Once the turkey starts to brown, sprinkle the taco seasoning (or your own mix of spices) over the meat. Stir well and cook for about 6-8 minutes, or until fully cooked (no pink left).
      -Add cheese: Once the turkey is cooked, sprinkle 1/2 cup of shredded cheese over the turkey and let it melt.

  3. Prepare the Toppings:
      -Chop the tomato: Dice the tomato into small pieces.
      -Shred lettuce: If its not pre-shredded, chop your lettuce into thin strips.
        -Heat taco shells: If you like your taco shells warm, microwave them for about 15-20 seconds on a microwave-safe plate.

    • Assemble the Tacos:
        -Drizzle with honey if you want it a bit sweeter. Add a squeeze of lime or lemon juice to keep the fruit fresh and to add a slight tang.

    • Optional Creamy Version:
        -Fill the shells: Spoon some of the cooked ground turkey with melted cheese into each taco shell.
        - Add toppings: Layer on your shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and more shredded cheese.
        -Drizzle with taco sauce: Add taco sauce for extra flavor.
        Finish with sour cream: Top it off with a dollop of sour cream!

    • Quick Tips:
        -Make extra: You can save leftovers for another meal the next day.
        -Customizable: Add avocado, onions, or jalapenos if you like!
        Store-bought taco kits: They come with shells, seasoning, and sauce for even faster prep.


These tacos are quick, tasty, and great for a busy college schedule! Enjoy!
