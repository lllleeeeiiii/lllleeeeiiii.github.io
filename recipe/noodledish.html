Noodle Dish


Heres a simple step-by-step recipe for a quick noodle dish thats perfect for a busy college student!:


Ingredients:

  • 1 package of instant noodles (like ramen, udon, or any noodles of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon of oil(vegetable or olive oil)
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables(frozen or fresh; examples: bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic (minced) or 1 teaspoon of garlic powder (optional)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of soy sauce(or to taste)
  • Salt and pepper(to taste)
  • Optional toppings:cooked protein (like chicken, shrimp, or tofu), green onions, sesame seeds, or hot sauce

Materials:

  • Pot for boiling water
  • Colander or strainer (for draining noodles)
  • Large skillet or pan
  • Wooden spoon or spatula
  • Bowl for serving
  • Measuring cup (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil the Noodles:
      -Boil water: Fill a pot with enough water to cover the noodles and bring it to a boil over high heat.
      - Add noodles: Once the water is boiling, add the instant noodles and cook according to the package instructions (usually about 3-5 minutes).
      Drain: Once cooked, carefully drain the noodles using a colander or strainer and set them aside.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables
      -Heat oil: In a large skillet or pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
      -Add vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables to the pan. If using fresh vegetables, saute them for about 3-5 minutes until they are tender. If using frozen, cook until heated through.
      Add garlic: If using minced garlic, add it to the pan and cook for an additional minute until fragrant (be careful not to burn it).

  3. Combine Noodles and Vegetables:
      -Add noodles: Once the vegetables are cooked, add the drained noodles to the skillet.
      - Add sauce: Pour in the soy sauce and stir everything together using a wooden spoon or spatula. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the noodles to soak up the sauce and heat through.

  4. Season and Serve:
      - Taste and adjust: Taste the noodle dish and add salt, pepper, or more soy sauce as needed.
      Serve: Transfer the noodles to a bowl. If you want to add any optional toppings, sprinkle them on top.

  5. Enjoy!:
      6. Dig in: Enjoy your quick and easy noodle dish!

  6. Tips:
      7. -Customize: Feel free to add any proteins you like, such as cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu, for a more filling meal.
      -Spice it up: Add hot sauce or chili flakes if you like some heat.
      -Quick meal prep: You can pre-cook some proteins or veggies on a weekend to make this meal even quicker on busy days.

Enjoy your delicious noodle dish!
